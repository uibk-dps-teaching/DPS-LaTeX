\Cref{tbl:some_table} as a caption and can be referenced.

	\begin{tabularx}{0.75\textwidth}{Xrr}
		\toprule
		Description & Count & Price (single)\\
		\midrule
		Soda & 6 & 2.49 €\\
		Coffee & 2 & 3.19 €\\
		Sandwich & 1 & 5.39 €\\

	\caption{This table has a caption.}
